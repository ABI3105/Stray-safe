Donate to Help Stray Animals

Your generous donations help us rescue, care for, and find loving homes for stray animals. Every contribution makes a significant impact.

Why Donate?

  • Rescue Operations: Your donations enable us to conduct rescue missions, bringing stray animals to safety.
  • Medical Care: Funds go towards providing essential medical care, including vaccinations, surgeries, and treatments for illnesses and injuries.
  • Shelter: We provide a safe and comfortable environment for stray animals, ensuring they have a warm place to stay.
  • Food and Supplies: Donations help us purchase nutritious food and necessary supplies to keep our animals healthy and happy.
  • Adoption Programs: Support our efforts to find loving, permanent homes for our animals through adoption events and promotions.

How Your Donation Helps

Every dollar you donate goes directly to helping stray animals. Here’s how your contribution can make a difference:

  • Rs.10: Provides food for a cat or dog for one week.
  • Rs.25: Covers basic medical care for a newly rescued animal.
  • Rs.50: Funds a spay/neuter surgery, helping to control the stray population.
  • Rs.100: Supports emergency medical treatments for critically injured animals.
  • Rs.250: Contributes to the overall shelter maintenance and improvement projects.

Success Stories

Thanks to your support, we have successfully rescued and rehomed hundreds of animals. Here are a few heartwarming stories:

  • Jimmy: Found abandoned and injured, Jimmy received life-saving surgery and has now found a loving home.
  • Max: Rescued from a dangerous situation, Max was treated for his wounds and is now thriving with his new family.
  • Luna: Once a stray kitten, Luna was given medical care and socialization, leading to her adoption by a wonderful family.
